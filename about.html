About Lau Gar

Lau Gar Kung Fu was first established in the UK in 1961 and has become one of the most well known and successful styles in the UK.

For more information about the style visit: https://www.laugar-kungfu.com/style-laugar-origin

About Our Club

Legacy Lau Gar has been established for over 30 years, training people from all levels of fitness and knowledge. We specialise on developing your ability within Kung Fu and self-defence

For this style of Kung Fu, you do not need to be strong or fit, to begin as we will help you to work on your fitness and flexibility at your own pace, alongside teaching you the art of Kung Fu.

You will be taught self-defence as well as the art itself which will in turn help to boost self confidence and increase you level of fitness.
